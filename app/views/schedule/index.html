15:00-16:00
What FIWARE can do for the IoT
Oscar Lazaro (FITMAN)
Rasmus Blom (Grundfos Connect)
Gabriella Cattaneo (IDC)
Carlos Ralli (TID) – Thierry Nagellen (Orange) FIWARE IoT Chapter
Christoph Thuemmler (FISTAR)
Peter Friess (European Commission)

The disruptive impact on future businesses, citizens and society of the Internet of Things in an Internet of Everything is no longer disputed. Over the recent past, we have witnessed how companies of all sizes are embracing the IoT technologies and working on innovative business propositions. Moreover, connected communities and ecosystems are starting to emerge being supported not only by embedded system manufacturers such ARM but also by end users aiming at delivering value added services on top of their traditional businesses models and products. New computing paradigms such as Fog Computing provide an amenable support and a more effective exploitation capabilities of the features that IoT technology can leverage to people and businesses. The workshop aims at bringing together key stakeholders to share their experiences, lessons learned, plans in the FIWARE, IoT and open innovation ecosystem domains and jointly identify the opportunities that FIWARE and IoT open ecosystems will bring to business development in general and to future FIWARE web-entrepreneurs taking part in the FIWARE acceleration programme in particular.

09:00 – 17:00 | FIWARE HANDS-ON SHOWCASES AND DEMONSTRATIONS

List of Show Case Demos
FIWARE “Open APIs for Open Minds”
FIWARE is an innovative, open cloud-based infrastructure for cost-effective creation and delivery of Future Internet applications and services, at a scale not seen before:
● Try out FI-Lab (Future Internet Lab): the experimentation environment for anyone interested in FIWARE.
● Get guidance on how to make the most of FIWare, based on learning experience in the last period.
● Experience examples of the most amazing applications and services created on top of FIWARE.
XIFI “Experimental Infrastructures for the Future Internet”
The XIFI platform enables the Federation of European Infrastructures for FIWARE services and applications. Come to our booth and you will be able to discover more. Several demos will be available:
● How to build a FI-Lab node from scratch and connect it to FI-Lab
● How to monitor a FI-Lab node
● How to connect virtual networks across two FI-Lab nodes
● How to register a SaaS service in a FI-Lab node
FI-CONTENT 2 “Open media platforms to develop innovative applications & services”
FI-CONTENT 2 offers software modules based on FIWARE that SMEs and developers can use to create applications in 3 main domains:
Social connected TV | Smart city services | Pervasive games.
Various demonstrations of the FI-CONTENT 2 possibilities will be made:
● Interactive multi-screen content for HbbTV,
● Audio Fingerprinting for Social Connected TV
● Augmented reality games prototypes
● Web augmented reality games prototypes
● Smart City guide
FINESCE “Talking Energy: Future Internet Technologies in the Energy Sector”
The FINESCE exhibition shows the very latest results of implementing FIWARE technologies in the energy sector. FINESCE presents different controlling and managing interfaces such as
● the smart energy control app of the smart buildings in Hyllie/Malmo and Horsens,
● a monitoring app for energy consumption and production trialled at the energy market in Terni,
● a real-time monitoring app of the smart factory in Aachen,
● a control and monitoring app for virtual power plants and
● an interface for weather forecasting processing using Big Data clusters.
FI-STAR “Future Internet Social and technological alignment in HEALTHCARE”
FI-STAR has established applications in the Health Care domain building on FIWARE technology:
● Presenting eHealth Apps including Diabetes Diary, Shock Treatment, RFID in Surgery
● Demo of use case trials, including demo surgery room and RFID hospital system
● Presentation of Modular Medical Architectures and Virtualization of Care in a Future Internet
● Presentation of 7 healthcare use cases through online media content
FITMAN – Future Internet Platforms for Smart, Digital and Virtual Factories
The FITMAN booth will host 6 industrial video / real life demonstrations of the uptake of FIWARE technologies into Smart-Digital-Virtual Factory scenarios. Several manufacturing business processes and sectors will be exhibited, such as
● automotive
● textile
● construction
● white goods
The FITMAN booth will also distribute dissemination material and provide information to FIWARE accelerators and proposers on how to access and use its platforms and services.
